Colors

Black, ivory, and rationed hue

Pitch-black stage, a blue-gray ink ramp, one ivory accent, the two duel/team hues, and a small status set (amber timer-warn, crit red, HP pink). Saturated color is rationed to team identity, the ivory accent, and status; nothing else. Each swatch's tonal ramp (dark to light, same hue/chroma) is synthesized in OKLCH, useful if a state needs a lighter or darker step of the same color later.

Typography

Hero headline over HUD readout

Archivo carries the one thing that matters (the title, the button labels). Martian Mono carries everything instrumentational — annotations, hints, the room code. Only the title's letter-spacing ever animates.

Elevation

Glow, not shadow

No card shadows, no panel elevation — the surface stays flat black. Depth reads as light: a two-layer text-shadow halo on the title, a soft bloom on the primary button's hover, a white focus ring on every interactive element.

Config Controls

The arena config panel

The densest surface in the app: mode pickers, sliders, steppers, and toggles. These stay strictly on the DESIGN.md palette. Selected and active states borrow landing's confident-on-commit ivory (primary-ivory); hover and focus are white-alpha washes, the same treatment landing uses on its ghost buttons. No new hues. Every control is interactive; each ships with its label.

Selector

Side-by-side segmented options, one active at a time. The active segment fills with the accent tint and a hairline ring. Supports an optional sublabel. Click to switch.
